About Me

I currently work as an Associate Front-End Developer at Internet Brands. I completed my Master's in Computer Science. My undergrad is in Computer Engineering. My dream has always been to work as a Web Developer for an IT company, so that I can utilize my programming as well as designing skills effectively for the success of the company and myself. During my undergrad years I developed websites for academic projects and I loved it. This motivated me in pursuing Master's in Computer Science to further enhance my knowledge.

Although I have only 2 year of professional work experience in IT yet, I learnt high-level programming languages currently used by leading IT companies.

I believe that I am intrinsically motivated to take up new responsibilities and roles. I have high self-efficacy levels, which is much like having an inner cheer leading squad that tells you will take on a particular task head on and do it well.
